Translation rules

Translation rules take into account not only a particular SBGN brick but also the surrounding connected bricks - contextual network information. By active entity we mean an entity that participate in following steps of the pathway/network. It is a glyph that has outgoing regulatory arcs (catalysis, stimulation, inhibition) or connected to other processes via a consumption arc.

Stimulation of irreversible metabolic reaction
SBGN-ML Newt SBGN-ML Newt
Macromolecule A stimulates reaction B to C

Inhibition of irreversible metabolic reaction
SBGN-ML Newt SBGN-ML Newt
Macromolecule A inhibits reaction B to C

Activation of protein phosphorylation: both states are inactive (dead end) - assumed active phosphorylated state
SBGN-ML Newt SBGN-ML Newt
A stimulates modification of macromolecule B
IMPORTANT: not obvious rule and potentially wrong translation
